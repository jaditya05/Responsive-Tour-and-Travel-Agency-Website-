Discover Northern India

In the heart of northern India, Delhi thrives as a city where ancient history intertwines with modern progress, offering a journey that captivates every explorer.

From the towering skyscrapers of Connaught Place to the historic grandeur of Red Fort, Delhi offers a seamless blend of modernity and heritage. Whether you’re indulging in its vibrant street food, exploring its bustling markets, immersing yourself in its rich arts and culture, or experiencing its dynamic nightlife, Delhi invites you to a city where history meets progress, and every moment is a new discovery.

Must-Visit Historical Sites in Northern India

Lotus Temple

★★★★★
(3,500 reviews)

The Lotus Temple is a Bahá'í House of Worship, famous for its stunning lotus-like architectural design. It offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere for visitors of all faiths to meditate and reflect.

Hawa Mahal

★★★★½
(4,000 reviews)

Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, is an iconic structure in Jaipur, known for its unique honeycomb architecture. It was built to allow royal women to observe street life without being seen.

Golden Temple

★★★★★
(5,200 reviews)

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is a revered spiritual site for Sikhs. Its stunning golden architecture and serene surroundings attract millions of visitors annually.
