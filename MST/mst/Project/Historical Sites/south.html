Discover Southern India

In the heart of northern India, Delhi thrives as a city where ancient history intertwines with modern progress, offering a journey that captivates every explorer.

From the towering skyscrapers of Connaught Place to the historic grandeur of Red Fort, Delhi offers a seamless blend of modernity and heritage. Whether you’re indulging in its vibrant street food, exploring its bustling markets, immersing yourself in its rich arts and culture, or experiencing its dynamic nightlife, Delhi invites you to a city where history meets progress, and every moment is a new discovery.

Must-Visit Historical Sites in Southern India

Mysore Palace

★★★★★
(3,500 reviews)

Mysore Palace, a stunning blend of Hindu, Mughal, Rajput, and Gothic styles, is one of India's most magnificent royal residences. Visitors are captivated by its opulent interiors, sprawling courtyards, and grand architecture.

Charminar

★★★★½
(4,000 reviews)

The Charminar, a symbol of Hyderabad's rich history, is an iconic 16th-century mosque known for its unique four grand arches and minarets, standing tall in the heart of the old city.

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

★★★★★
(5,200 reviews)

The Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, located in Thiruvananthapuram, is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Its grand architecture, intricate carvings, and spiritual significance make it a must-visit pilgrimage site.
