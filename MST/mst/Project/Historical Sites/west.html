Discover Western India

Western India is home to some of the most awe-inspiring historical sites that reflect the rich spiritual and architectural heritage of the region. Whether you're exploring the awe-inspiring Ajanta Caves, the sacred Somnath Temple, or the majestic Rani Ki Vav, each site offers a unique glimpse into India's past.

The region combines ancient rock-cut architecture with a rich history of kings and kingdoms, making it one of India's most captivating cultural hotspots. A journey through Western India is a journey through time, filled with art, faith, and history.

Must-Visit Historical Sites in Western India

Ajanta Caves

★★★★★
(5,000 reviews)

The Ajanta Caves are a series of ancient rock-cut Buddhist cave monuments, famous for their exquisite paintings, sculptures, and historical significance. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, these caves date back to the 2nd century BC.

Somnath Temple

★★★★½
(4,200 reviews)

The Somnath Temple, located in Gujarat, is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva. It is renowned for its rich history and stunning coastal architecture, drawing pilgrims and visitors from around the world.

Rani Ki Vav

★★★★★
(4,800 reviews)

Rani Ki Vav, a stepwell located in Patan, Gujarat, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning architecture and intricate sculptures. Built during the 11th century, it serves as a beautiful example of ancient water management and craftsmanship.
