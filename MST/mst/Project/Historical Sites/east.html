Discover Eastern India

Eastern India is home to a wealth of historical and architectural wonders that reflect the spiritual and royal heritage of the region. From grand temples to colonial-era buildings, this region offers a deep dive into India's diverse history.

Whether you're marveling at the intricate carvings of Konark Sun Temple, admiring the sacred Lingaraj Temple, or basking in the grandeur of the Victoria Memorial, Eastern India invites you to explore a world where ancient traditions and colonial influences co-exist in harmony.

Must-Visit Historical Sites in Eastern India

Konark Sun Temple

★★★★★
(4,500 reviews)

The Konark Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an architectural marvel dedicated to the Sun God. Its intricate stone carvings and the chariot-shaped temple leave visitors awestruck by its grandeur and engineering brilliance.

Lingaraj Temple

★★★★½
(3,800 reviews)

The Lingaraj Temple in Bhubaneswar is one of the largest and oldest temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. With its exquisite Kalinga architecture, it remains an important pilgrimage site for Hindus.

Victoria Memorial

★★★★★
(5,200 reviews)

The Victoria Memorial in Kolkata, an iconic symbol of British rule, blends classical European architecture with Mughal influences. It stands as a tribute to Queen Victoria and a testament to the British colonial era in India.
