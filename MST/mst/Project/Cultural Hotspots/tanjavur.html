Discover Thanjavur

Thanjavur, known as the 'Rice Bowl of Tamil Nadu', is celebrated for its grand temples, classical music, and iconic paintings.

From the architectural brilliance of the Brihadeeswarar Temple to the vibrant culture of Tanjore paintings, Thanjavur stands as a beacon of South Indian heritage and artistry.
