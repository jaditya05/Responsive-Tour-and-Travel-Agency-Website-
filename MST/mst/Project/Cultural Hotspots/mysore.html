Discover Mysore

Mysore, known for its palatial architecture and rich history, is a city that blends tradition with cultural vibrance.

From the iconic Mysore Palace to the serene Brindavan Gardens, Mysore offers an enchanting experience with its historic sites, thriving art scene, and grand festivities, especially during the world-famous Dasara celebrations.
