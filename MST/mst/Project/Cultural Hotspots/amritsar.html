Discover Amritsar

Amritsar, the spiritual and cultural hub of Punjab, is renowned for its iconic Golden Temple and historic landmarks.

From the Wagah Border ceremony to the historic Jallianwala Bagh, Amritsar offers a unique blend of history, culture, and spirituality that captivates visitors.
