Discover Darjeeling

In the foothills of the Himalayas, Darjeeling flourishes as a town where colonial charm blends with breathtaking natural beauty, offering a journey that captivates every explorer.

Nestled in the lap of the Eastern Himalayas, Darjeeling is a serene retreat where misty hills, lush tea gardens, and colonial-era architecture come together in perfect harmony. Known for its spectacular sunrise views over the majestic Kanchenjunga, this charming hill station offers a unique blend of adventure and tranquility. From the iconic toy train ride to the peaceful monasteries, every corner of Darjeeling invites you to explore its rich cultural heritage and natural splendor. Whether you’re trekking through pine forests, sipping tea amidst the rolling hills, or soaking in the stunning vistas, Darjeeling promises an unforgettable experience.

Must-Visit Places in Darjeeling

Tea Gardens

★★★★★
(4,684 reviews)

Darjeeling’s tea gardens are a sight to behold, stretching across the rolling hills in a breathtaking patchwork of green. These lush plantations are famous for producing the world-renowned Darjeeling tea, known for its delicate flavor and unique aroma. Visitors can explore the tea estates, such as Makaibari, Happy Valley, and Singtom, where they can witness the traditional tea-making process.

Tiger Hill

★★★★½
(3,184 reviews)

Tiger Hill in Darjeeling is one of the most famous and sought-after destinations for sunrise views. Located at an elevation of 2,590 meters, it offers a stunning panoramic view of the snow-capped peaks of the Kanchenjunga range, with the first rays of sunlight illuminating the mountains in a golden glow. The view of the Kanchenjunga, the third-highest peak in the world, is truly a sight to behold and is often considered one of the best sunrise points in India.

Mirik Lake

★★★★★
(4,892reviews)

Mirik Lake, nestled in the tranquil town of Mirik, is one of Darjeeling's most serene and picturesque spots. Surrounded by lush green hills and dense pine forests, this beautiful lake offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely boat ride on the calm waters, taking in the stunning views of the surrounding landscape and the majestic Kanchenjunga in the distance.
