Discover Ooty

In the heart of the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty flourishes as a town where colonial charm blends with lush greenery and serene lakes, offering a journey that captivates every traveler.

In the heart of the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty is a tranquil retreat where rolling tea estates, mist-covered valleys, and colonial-era charm blend in perfect harmony. Known for its picturesque landscapes and cool, refreshing climate, this enchanting hill station offers a unique mix of relaxation and adventure. From the historic Nilgiri Mountain Railway to the serene Ooty Lake, every corner of Ooty invites you to immerse yourself in its natural beauty and rich heritage. Whether you're strolling through vibrant botanical gardens, boating on crystal-clear lakes, or admiring panoramic views from Doddabetta Peak, Ooty promises an unforgettable escape into nature’s embrace.

Must-Visit Places in Ooty

Doddabetta Peak

★★★★★
(3,824 reviews)

Doddabetta Peak, the highest point in the Nilgiri Hills at 2,637 meters, offers breathtaking panoramic views of Ooty and its surroundings. Located 10 km from Ooty town, it is a popular spot for trekking and nature lovers. The peak features a Telescope House for visitors to enjoy a closer view of the stunning landscapes. Surrounded by dense shola forests, it is home to diverse flora and fauna. With its cool climate and misty charm, Doddabetta Peak is a must-visit destination in Ooty.

Avalanche Lake

★★★★½
(2,715 reviews)

Avalanche Lake, located around 28 km from Ooty, is a serene and picturesque destination surrounded by lush green forests and rolling hills. The lake is known for its calm waters and stunning views, making it perfect for nature walks, picnics, and photography. It is also a popular spot for adventure activities like trekking and camping. The area is rich in biodiversity, with unique flora and fauna.

Marina Beach

★★★★★
(5,893 reviews)

Marina Beach, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, stretching over 13 km along the Bay of Bengal. Known for its golden sands, gentle sea breeze, and vibrant atmosphere, it is a popular destination for both locals and tourists. The beach is lined with historic monuments, including the Anna Memorial and the M.G.R. Memorial, adding a cultural touch to the natural beauty.
